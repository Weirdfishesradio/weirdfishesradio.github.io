about gnawed

gnawed is a one-person brand created and run by a high school student. every garment is altered, distressed, and prepared by hand.

this project began as a way to reclaim forgotten clothing — to make something delicate out of damage.

a portion of profits are donated to One Tree Planted , supporting global reforestation and environmental restoration.

nothing here is mass-produced. nothing is perfect. everything is intentional.
