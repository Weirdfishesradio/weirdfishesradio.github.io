about gnawed

gnawed is a one-person brand created and run by a high school student. every garment is altered, reconstructed, and prepared by hand.

the brand began as a way to reclaim forgotten clothing — to soften damage and turn it into something intentional.

a portion of profits are donated to One Tree Planted , supporting global reforestation and environmental restoration.

nothing here is mass-produced. nothing is perfect. everything is made slowly.
